What Is AfterWords

Words that carry on long after we are gone

AfterWords is a platform for preserving voice messages — birthdays, anniversaries, milestones, daily moments — and delivering them to the people who matter most, on the days that matter most, even after you're no longer here.

It is the most personal gift a person can leave behind. Not a photo. Not a letter. A voice.

The Two Vaults

Two ways to hold on

I
🕯️

Legacy Vault

For those who want to leave something behind. Record voice messages now — for future birthdays, graduations, weddings, or simply a quiet Tuesday — and trust AfterWords to deliver them when the time comes.

  • Schedule delivery for any future date
  • Milestone-triggered messages
  • Unlimited recordings stored securely
  • Delivered via voicemail, audio file, or app
  • Trusted contact manages your vault
II
🤍

Grief Vault

For those who have already lost someone. If your loved one recorded messages before they passed, the Grief Vault holds them safely — releasing them at the moments they intended, or whenever you need to hear them most.

  • Receive messages from those you've lost
  • AI-reviewed for emotional safety
  • Notifications before delivery — never a surprise
  • Share recordings with family members
  • Lifetime archive, never deleted
How It Works

Simple enough to do today

01
Create Your Vault

Sign up and create your personal AfterWords vault. Add a trusted contact — someone who will help manage delivery when the time comes. Takes less than five minutes.

02
Record Your Messages

Record voice messages directly in the app. Speak to your children, your partner, your grandchildren, your best friend. Say what you've always meant to say. There are no limits.

03
Set Your Delivery

Choose when each message is delivered. A specific date. A milestone like a wedding or graduation. Or leave it to your trusted contact to release them at the right moment.

04
AfterWords Does the Rest

Your messages are stored safely, forever. When the time comes — whether that's tomorrow or twenty years from now — AfterWords delivers your voice, exactly as you intended.
